A quality prospect list is the foundation of B2B sales. Without it, you're doing cold outreach to the wrong people. Here's the process for building one that actually converts.

## Step 1: Define your ideal customer profile (ICP)

Before gathering a single contact, define exactly who you're targeting:

**Questions to answer:**
- What industry are they in? (Be specific: "manufacturing" is too broad; "food & beverage manufacturing with 10–100 employees" is actionable)
- Where are they located? (City, state, or region)
- What's their company size? (Employee count, revenue, office count)
- Who's the buyer? (Founder, Operations Manager, IT head, Marketing Director)
- What problem do they have that you solve? (This shapes your outreach message)

**Example ICP:** "Operations managers at logistics companies in Lagos with 20+ employees and a social media presence indicating active operations"

## Step 2: Choose your data sources

For Nigerian businesses:

**Google Maps:** Best for local, service-based businesses. Dental clinics, restaurants, hotels, law firms, logistics companies. Listings include phone, website, hours, reviews.

**LinkedIn:** Best for professional services, enterprise, technology companies. Company pages include employee count, industry, and you can often find individual contacts.

**VConnect Nigeria:** Directory with category browsing. Useful for manufacturing and trade.

**Nairaland Business Directory:** SME listings.

**FIRS/CAC company registry:** For businesses registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission — publicly accessible.

**Industry associations:** NECA (employers), MAN (manufacturers), NACCIMA (chambers of commerce) — member directories when available.

**Company websites:** For enrichment after initial list — contact pages, team pages, social links.

## Step 4: Verify and enrich

A raw scraped list needs cleaning:
- **Email verification:** Remove invalid and bounced addresses before sending. 15–25% of scraped emails will be invalid.
- **Deduplication:** Remove duplicate companies
- **Enrichment:** Add missing data — company size from website signals, LinkedIn profile, recent news

HarvestAI's Enrich mode takes a list and adds missing fields where available.

## Step 5: Score and prioritize

Not all leads are equal. Score based on:
- Decision-maker access (direct name and email vs. info@)
- Business signals (active website, social media, recent activity)
- Fit with your ICP (how closely they match)
- Timing signals (recently hired, recently funded, recently expanded)

Work the top 20% of your list intensively before moving to the broader list.
