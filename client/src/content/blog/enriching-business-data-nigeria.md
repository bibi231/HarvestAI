## What Enrichment Means in Practice

Data enrichment is the process of taking a record with incomplete information and appending additional fields from secondary sources. The starting point is typically a company name (or domain). The output is a fuller record with the fields your sales and marketing teams need to work effectively.

A typical enrichment run turns:

**Before**: `Reliable Agro Supplies, Lagos`

**After**: `Reliable Agro Supplies | reliableagro.com.ng | Agricultural inputs distributor | Mushin, Lagos | 45–80 employees | CEO: Emeka Okafor | emeka@reliableagro.com.ng | +234 803 XXX XXXX | ₦200M–500M annual revenue`

That transformation is the difference between a list you can browse and a list you can work.

## The Most Valuable Fields to Enrich

For Nigerian B2B prospecting, prioritise enriching:

### Company-Level Fields
- **Industry and sub-sector** (not just "manufacturing" but "FMCG personal care manufacturing")
- **Website domain** — essential for email pattern matching and research
- **Physical address** with LGA and state
- **Employee count range**
- **Year of incorporation** (from CAC data, indicates maturity)

### Contact-Level Fields
- **Decision-maker name and title**
- **Direct email address** (verified)
- **Direct phone number**
- **LinkedIn profile URL**

### Intent and Context Fields
- **Recent funding or acquisition events**
- **Active job postings** (reveals growth areas and strategic priorities)
- **Technology stack** (what software they currently use — signals compatibility or displacement opportunity)

## Enrichment Sources in the Nigerian Context

| Source | Best For |
|--------|----------|
| CAC records | Legal name, registration date, directors |
| LinkedIn | Individual contacts, company size, current roles |
| VConnect / Kompass | Phone numbers, general company info |
| Company websites | Products, team pages, direct contact details |
| Google Maps | Operating status, customer reviews, address |
| Jumia/Konga seller profiles | For product-selling businesses |
| Press releases / TechCabal / BusinessDay | Funding, expansion, leadership changes |

No single source covers everything. Enrichment pipelines typically hit 3–5 sources per record.

## Prioritising Which Records to Enrich

Enrichment takes time and (if you're using data services) costs money. Not every record in your database deserves full enrichment. Apply enrichment effort proportionally:

- **High-fit targets**: Full enrichment — company, contact, intent signals
- **Medium-fit targets**: Company-level enrichment only; contact enrichment on request
- **Low-fit targets**: Verify they're still active; do not invest further

## Enrichment Is Not a One-Time Activity

Company data decays. Research suggests that B2B contact data has an accuracy half-life of roughly 12–18 months — people change jobs, companies restructure, phone numbers change. In Nigeria, where business volatility is higher than in more stable economies, the decay is faster.

A useful rule: re-enrich any record that has been sitting untouched in your CRM for more than 12 months before your sales team acts on it.

## The Payoff

Teams that sell from enriched, verified lists consistently report:

- Lower bounce rates on email outreach
- Higher connect rates on calls (direct lines vs. switchboards)
- Shorter discovery calls (context gathered pre-call)
- Higher close rates from better-qualified conversations

Enrichment is an investment in sales efficiency. The time saved on dead-end outreach typically pays back the enrichment cost within the first campaign.
