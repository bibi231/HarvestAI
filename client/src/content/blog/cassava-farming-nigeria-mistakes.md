Every B2B contact list gets worse over time. People leave companies, change roles, switch phone numbers, and retire email addresses. In Nigeria, where business volatility is relatively high, the decay rate is faster than most markets.

## The data decay problem by the numbers

Research across B2B databases shows:
- 25–30% of email addresses become invalid within 12 months
- 45% of professionals change job or title within 2 years
- 35% of phone numbers change or are abandoned annually in Nigeria
- A 3-year-old contact list may have only 40–50% valid records

If you're sending outreach to a 2-year-old list of 1,000 contacts:
- 300 emails will bounce (hard or soft)
- 200 more contacts will no longer be decision-makers at their listed companies
- Your effective reaching rate drops from 1,000 to ~500 valid, relevant contacts

This impacts sender reputation (high bounce rates cause spam flagging), and wastes time on unqualified prospects.

## What data enrichment does

Data enrichment takes your existing contact list and:
1. Verifies which emails are still valid
2. Adds missing data fields (phone, LinkedIn, company size, website)
3. Updates changed fields where new data is publicly available
4. Flags records that appear outdated for review or removal

## What fields matter most for Nigerian B2B

**For outreach quality:**
- Current email (verified)
- Current phone (WhatsApp-enabled is worth noting)
- Current role / title at current company
- LinkedIn profile URL

**For personalization:**
- Company size (signals buying capacity)
- Recent company news or growth signals
- Active website (signals engaged business)
- Geographic location (specific LGA or city area)

**For routing:**
- Decision-maker level (C-suite, manager, analyst)
- Buying authority for your product category

**Step 5:** Schedule re-enrichment every 6 months

## Preventing decay: active data hygiene

Beyond periodic enrichment, habits that slow decay:
- Update records immediately when you get an email bounce (mark, don't just delete)
- Add a "last verified" field and sort by it
- When prospects reply to outreach, verify and update their info
- Ask for business cards or contacts after every meeting or event

## The ROI of enriched data

A 1,000-contact list with 70% valid emails vs. 40% valid emails means:
- 70% list: 700 deliverable emails, ~84 responses at 12% rate
- 40% list: 400 deliverable emails, ~48 responses at 12% rate

The difference: 36 additional conversations from the same list, same effort. At an average deal value of ₦200,000, that's ₦7.2 million in additional pipeline from data hygiene.
