Getting an email address for the right person at a Nigerian company is harder than it looks. info@ addresses go nowhere. Finding the actual decision-maker's email requires either a tool, a referral, or a method.

## Why email finding is harder in Nigeria

In Western markets, professional email addresses are often publicly listed on LinkedIn or company websites. Nigerian professionals are less likely to list their direct emails publicly — for good reason (spam is high).

Additionally, many Nigerian companies use generic email formats (info@, contact@, admin@) as their public-facing addresses. The decision-maker uses a personal corporate email that isn't listed anywhere obvious.

## Method 1: Company email pattern guessing

Most companies use a consistent email format:
- firstname.lastname@company.com
- firstname@company.com
- f.lastname@company.com
- lastname.firstname@company.com

**How to identify the pattern:**
1. Find any email from the company (even one in a press release or LinkedIn comment)
2. Use that email to infer the company's pattern
3. Apply the pattern to the name of your target contact

**Verification:** Use email verification tools (HarvestAI's Enrich mode, or free tools like NeverBounce, ZeroBounce) to check if the constructed email is valid before sending.

## Method 2: LinkedIn + company website

Many Nigerian professionals list email addresses in their LinkedIn "Contact info" section (visible to connections). Connection request + message often gets a response that includes or leads to an email.

Company website "About" and "Team" pages sometimes list individual emails, especially at smaller companies.

## Email verification matters

Before any outreach campaign, verify all email addresses:
- **Soft bounce risk:** Email exists but is overloaded or blocking
- **Hard bounce:** Email address doesn't exist
- **Catch-all domain:** Server accepts all emails regardless of validity

Sending to unverified lists damages your sender reputation, which causes your emails to land in spam — making your whole outreach program less effective.

Target: send only to addresses with 80%+ confidence score and verified-not-bounced status.
